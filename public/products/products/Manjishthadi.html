  1. Preparation: Begin by mixing the powder from the smaller sachet with 450ml of water in a bowl. Bring this mixture to a boil.
  2. Cooling and Blending: Once boiled, strain the mixture into another bowl (preferably made of iron) and allow it to cool until it reaches a lukewarm temperature. Add the powder from the larger sachet and let it sit for 15 to 24 hours to activate.
  3. Application: Apply the prepared mixture to the scalp and hair using a brush, similar to the application process of henna. Ensure an even coverage and leave it on for 3 to 3.5 hours.
  4. Rinsing: After the application time has elapsed, rinse the hair thoroughly with warm water only. Shampooing is recommended the next day, especially after applying oil to the hair, for best results.
