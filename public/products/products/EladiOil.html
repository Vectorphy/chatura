Eladi Oil

Eladi Oil is a deeply moisturizing body and face oil formulated to combat extreme dryness, skin itchiness, and various skin issues. This Ayurvedic blend addresses patches of extra dry skin and helps treat fungal patches, leaving your skin feeling soft, rejuvenated, and well-hydrated. The unique combination of natural oils and Ayurvedic herbs nourishes the skin, promoting overall skin health and a balanced complexion.

Kushta, Nagkeshar, Black cardamom, Saffron, Guggul, Lotus, Sesame oil, Coconut oil, Almond oil.
  • Deep Moisturization: The blend of Sesame, Coconut, and Almond oils penetrates deeply into the skin, providing long-lasting hydration and nourishment.
  • Soothes and Calms: Ingredients like Kushta and Lotus help to calm irritated skin, reducing redness and itchiness associated with dryness.

  1. Apply Eladi Oil generously to your body and face before bathing.
  2. Leave it on for 15-120 minutes to allow the oil to absorb and nourish the skin.
  3. For areas of extreme dryness, reapply as needed throughout the day.
  4. Wash off with mild soap and lukewarm water.

Caution:

  • For external use only.
  • Perform a patch test before use. Discontinue if irritation occurs.
  • Store in a cool, dry place.
  • Consult a dermatologist if you have allergies or sensitive skin.
The ingredients in Eladi Oil are processed using traditional Ayurvedic extraction methods to preserve their therapeutic properties. This includes cold pressing and infusing herbs in natural oils, ensuring that the active components remain potent and effective.

Q: What is Eladi Oil?

A: Eladi Oil is a traditional Ayurvedic blend formulated to deeply moisturize and heal dry, itchy skin. It addresses various skin issues and helps treat fungal patches, leaving skin soft, rejuvenated, and hydrated.

Q: What are the main uses of Eladi Oil?

A: Eladi Oil is used for moisturizing dry and flaky skin, reducing itchiness and irritation, and rehydrating patches of extra-dry skin. It is also used daily as a pre-bath treatment and reapplied throughout the day for maintained moisture levels.

Q: Is Eladi Oil suitable for all skin types?

A: While generally suitable for dry to normal skin types, those with very sensitive skin or pre-existing skin conditions should consult a dermatologist before use. A patch test is also recommended.
